Short Bio

Aleksei is a Ph.D. student at IST Austria, currently working with Chris Wojtan.

His research interests broadly lie in physically-based modelling and animation, deep learning and design of efficient algorithms and data structures.

Throught his studies he has done Deep Learning Research and Software Engineering interships at NVIDIA and Google, working on parts of YouTube, Maps and Android products. His work was supported by the Ilya Segalovich Scholarship in 2017 and 2018.

Aleksei has obtained a M.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Mathematics with Honors from Skoltech, advised by Sergey Rykovanov. He has received a B.Sc. degree with Honors in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Higher School of Economics, where his advisor was Anton Konushin.
