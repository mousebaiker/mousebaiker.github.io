Short Bio

Aleksei is a Ph.D. student at IST Austria, currently rotating with Chris Wojtan.

His research interests broadly lie in physically-based modelling and animation, deep learning and design of efficient algorithms and data structures.

In spring and summer 2021 he has done a Deep Learning Research Internship in Boris Ginsburg's team at NVIDIA, focusing on automatic speech recognition. Prior to that, he has done five interships (summers 2016 - 2020) at Google, working on parts of YouTube, Maps and Android products.

Aleksei has obtained a M.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Mathematics with Honors from Skoltech, advised by Sergey Rykovanov. He has received a B.Sc. degree with Honors in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Higher School of Economics, where his advisor was Anton Konushin. His work was supported by the Ilya Segalovich Scholarship in 2017 and 2018.
